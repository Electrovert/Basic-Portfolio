About Me

Web development did not come naturally to me, instead, it came to me one day while editing a training document at work. I realized that I had absolutely no idea how any of the internet worked; never mind how the HTML I was writing affected the page I was editing. This created a thirst for more information. Though I did find many online coding courses, I could not connect to what I was learning in a meaningful way. This is why I decided to come to UNH's Coding Bootcamp.



My main objective with web development is to help people in an impactful way.
Not reinventing the wheel by any means, instead I'd like to bridge the gap for individuals needing and not receiving mental health services.
